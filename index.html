      About Us

      NLearn - NSBM Learning Platform is implemented by NSBM Green University with the objective of enhancing the engagement of the NSBM community and streamlining its teaching and learning process. It also serves as the connective platform between NSBM Academic staff and the students in distance learning by providing access to study materials and resources, timetables, announcements, assessments, and other supplementary aids and tools.
